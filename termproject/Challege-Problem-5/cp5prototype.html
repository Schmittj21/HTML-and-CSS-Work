Hunting

A year later, I was invited to Argentina to go dove hunting with friends. We shot for five days, leaving the evening aftera morning hunt. This trip marked the start of my love for hunting. In the evenings, we were met with an elaborate dinner. Somtimes this meant a smoked pig, doves fresh from the field, or some other variety of native Argentinian food, specifically form the Cordoba region.
